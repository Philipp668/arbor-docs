# Bonds vs loans

Bank loans are the standard way small to medium businesses borrow money. The problem that large organizations run into is that they typically need far more money than the average bank can provide.

Bonds provide a solution by allowing many individual investors to assume the role of the lender. Indeed, public debt markets let thousands of investors each lend a portion of the capital needed.
